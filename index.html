About WineCheck

WineCheck uses AI to analyze wine labels and tell you if you're getting a good deal. Simply scan any wine label, enter the price, and get instant insights including critic ratings, tasting notes, and food pairings.

How It Works

  1. Scan - Point your camera at any wine label
  2. Enter Price - Tell us how much you're being asked to pay
  3. Get Insights - Receive instant analysis: GREAT VALUE, FAIR PRICE, OVERPRICED, or SKIP IT
