WineCheck Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

WineCheck ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our wine scanning application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access: WineCheck uses your device's camera solely to scan wine labels. Images are processed temporarily and are not stored on our servers.

No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

How We Use Information

Wine label images are sent to our AI service (OpenAI) for analysis. This processing is done in real-time, and images are not retained after the analysis is complete.

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API to analyze wine labels. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data processed through their service.

Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS protocols.

Children's Privacy

WineCheck is intended for users of legal drinking age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
